# HTML -  Desafio 1

<!DOCTYPE HTML>
<html>
    <head>
        <title>Desafio: Escreva um Poema </title>
        <meta charset="utf-8">
    </head>
    <body>
    <h1>Desafio: Escreva um Poema</h1>
    <h2>Diário</h2>
    <p>
    Tudo começou quando o sol me abraçou <br>
    Dizendo bom dia,<br>
    Como vai a vida?<br>
    E disse-lhe: do jeito que eu queria! <br>
    <br>
    Café quente na mesa,<br>
    Uma página em branco,<br>
    Um sorriso para quebrar a tristeza <br>
    De quem no caminho encontro.<br>
    <br>
    
    </p>
    
    
    </body>
</html>

# Destaques em textos
<!DOCTYPE HTML>
<html>
    <head>
        <title>Challenge: You can learn text tags</title>
        <meta charset="utf-8">
    </head>
    <body>
        <p>
        Nobody’s born <em>smart</em>. We all start at 0. Can’t talk, can’t walk, <strong>certainly can’t do algebra.</strong><br>
        Adding, reading, writing, riding a bike. Nobody’s good at anything at first.<br>
        There was a time when Einstein couldn’t <strong>count to 10.</strong><br>
        And Shakespeare had to learn his <em>ABCs</em> just like the rest of us.<br>
        Thankfully, we are born to learn.<br>
        Slowly. Surely. You stumble, slip, crawl, fall and fail and fall.<br>
        Frustrating. Confusing. Trying. Struggling.<br>
        Until one day, you walk.<br>
        One foot in front of the other. One idea on top of the next.<br>
        Each wrong answer making your brain a little bit stronger.<br>
        Failing is just another word for growing. And you keep going.<br>
        This. is. learning.<br>
        It’s not that you don’t get it. <br>
        You just don’t get it, yet.
        Because the most beautiful, complex concepts in the whole universe<br>
        are built on basic ideas that anyone, anywhere can understand.<br>
        Whoever you are, wherever you are.<br>
        </p>
        <p>
        You only have to know one thing:<br>
        <strong><em>You</em> <em>can</em> <em>learn</em> <em>anything.</em></strong>
        </p>
    </body>
</html>
